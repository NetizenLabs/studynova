# Stop Random Studying

Most students fail because they rely on motivation rather than systems. When you wake up and ask, "What should I study today?", you've already lost. 

A study schedule is not about restricting your freedom; it's about giving you the freedom to relax without guilt because you know exactly when your work will get done.

## The 3-Step System

### 1. The Brain Dump
Write down everything you need to do this week. Don't filter, just get it out of your head.

### 2. Time Blocking
Use a calendar app (like Google Calendar or Apple Calendar) to block out your non-negotiables:
- Classes
- Work shifts
- Commute time
- Sleep (8 hours minimum)

### 3. Study Blocks
Now, plug your brain dump tasks into the empty spaces. 
**Pro tip:** Use our [Study Timer](/tools/study-timer) during these blocks to maintain focus using the Pomodoro technique.

## Review and Adapt
At the end of the week, review what worked and what didn't. Did you underestimate how long reading would take? Adjust for next week. A system is only as good as its maintenance.
